  • Hall for Hire

    • Woodchurch, Kent

    The village has a beautiful green situated in the centre where you can see the ancient Church in the distance.

    There are several shops in the village such as the post office, butchers, general store, paper shop, and two friendly Pubs which serve delicious food and real ales.

    The museum and Windmill are open to the public most days and, if you have the time, visit the Rare Breeds Centre – it’s a great day out for children.

    Nearby towns and attractions

    There are lots of easily accessible place's of interest in the surrounding areas such as Canterbury Cathedral, National Trust Properties, the seaside and several shopping towns.

    Ashford (8 miles)

    The nearest main town of Ashford which also houses the Designer Outlet. excellent travel links with the motorway and rail services to London and Europe.

    More info: AshfordTourism.co.uk

    Tenterden (4 miles)

    The old town of Tenterden has a bustling High Street full of various little shops of interest with a bus service from Woodchurch and steam trains running from Tenterden to Bodium.

    More info: TenterdenTown.co.uk
